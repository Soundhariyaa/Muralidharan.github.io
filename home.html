BLUE Launcher for SIH (Hardware)


  • Quick analysis of quality of cereals, oil Seeds, pulses using AI

  • Selling the cultivated seeds stands a great pressure for the farmers after the harvesting of the crops. Nowadays the agricultural products are not priced depending on the quality as per its combination. The device BLUE is designed in such a way that the device checks for the quality of the seeds automatically on the click of each codes pre assigned to the particular specimen being feed into the device. Different compositions are being tested within a small interval of time automatically.
